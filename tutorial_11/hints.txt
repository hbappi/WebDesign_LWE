 Combining Diacritical Marks:

    - A diacritical mark is a "glyph" added to a letter.
    - Some diacritical marks, like grave (  ̀) and acute (  ́) are called accents.


 
 ̀	 a	a&#768;	à
 ́	 a	a&#769;	á
 ́	 O	O&#769;	Ó
 ̃	 O	O&#771;	Õ
